Nizhónígo Ííná

Telehealth platform · Cornell Engineering World Health

A community-centered digital wellness hub designed for the Navajo Nation, where over 50% of households lack broadband. The platform integrates clinical resources, culturally grounded knowledge, and local care information into one accessible space — blending digital health tools with Diné principles like Hózhó (balance) and K'é (kinship). I'm helping build the React.js interface around earth-tone palettes, circular layouts, and low-bandwidth, mobile-first design so it works in resource-limited settings.
